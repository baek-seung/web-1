Who Is Jesus Christ?

Jesus Christ is God incarnate, the second person of the Trinity, our creator, the resurrected savior and redeemer, and the only means of salvation for sinners.

Jesus of Nazareth was a Jewish carpenter and teacher in the first century A.D.
Jesus is the Christ or Messiah (Anointed One) of Israel.
He was born as God in the flesh, lived among men, and taught a “new covenant” in the Jewish tradition.
He was crucified by Roman authorities around A.D 30.
After he died, Jesus was buried and rose on the third day to prove his power over death.
His life, ministry, miracles, death, and resurrection point to his mission: Jesus Christ is both God and the Savior of the world.
