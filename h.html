Who Is Holy Spirit?

The Holy Spirit is one of the three persons within one divine Trinity.
The other two persons are God the Father and God the Son, Jesus Christ.
The Holy Spirit is a person, rather than a depersonalized force.
And the Holy Spirit is the agent by which Christians transform (2 Corinthians 3:18) and grow in holiness.
